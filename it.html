Information Technology


The Department of Information Technology has 5 labs equipped with over 120 computers networked using Ethernet. The IT labs are well equipped with software like Embedded systems Design: Keil RTOS, DSP Based design: MATLAB, Signal Processing Toolkit. Microcontroller Based Design: Microcontrollers Simulation Package RDBMS: Oracle 10g for Windows and Linux, SQL Server 2000 Image Processing: MATLAB Image Processing toolkit, Software Engineering & Project Management: IBM Rational Rose. M/s Bharti telecom have established a Professor Chair in telecommunication and Information Technology. Under the aegis of this chair, Bharti Research and Training Centre is being established.

The field of Information Technology (IT) in engineering encompasses the application of computer systems, software, and networks to solve complex problems and meet technological challenges. IT professionals play a vital role in designing, developing, implementing, and managing various information systems that enable organizations to operate efficiently and effectively in today's digital age. IT engineers possess a diverse skill set that includes programming, database management, network administration, cybersecurity, and system analysis. They are responsible for creating and maintaining software applications, managing data storage and retrieval, and ensuring the smooth functioning of computer networks. They work closely with other professionals to understand business requirements and develop innovative solutions to meet organizational needs. One of the key areas within IT is software development, where engineers design and build software applications for various purposes, such as web development, mobile app development, and enterprise software solutions. IT engineers also contribute to areas like artificial intelligence, data analytics, cloud computing, and Internet of Things (IoT), driving advancements and innovations across industries. The IT field offers a wide range of career opportunities in both technical and managerial roles. IT professionals can work in diverse sectors, including technology companies, consulting firms, financial institutions, healthcare organizations, government agencies, and more. With the rapid growth of digital transformation, the demand for IT professionals continues to increase, presenting numerous job prospects and growth potential. To succeed in the IT field, engineers must stay updated with the latest technologies and trends, continuously enhance their skills, and adapt to evolving industry standards. Effective problem-solving, analytical thinking, communication, and teamwork are crucial attributes for IT professionals to thrive in a dynamic and fast-paced environment. In summary, the IT branch in engineering is a dynamic and evolving field that plays a critical role in shaping our digital world. IT professionals contribute to developing innovative solutions, managing information systems, and leveraging technology to drive efficiency and productivity across industries. With its vast scope and promising career opportunities, the IT field remains a compelling choice for individuals passionate about technology and its transformative potential.
